Why Are Ice Glaciers Melting?

Due to the fact that human activites are increasing such as industrial revolution, carbon dioxide and greenhouse emissions, ice glaciers are melting at a very rapid pace. When ice glaciers melt, it increases sea level and increases the chances of typhoon as well as hurricanes. As humans, its important to do everything possible to try and avoid theses tragedies.

What Can We Do?

Humans can reduce global warming by: Switching to Renewable Energy, Reduce Energy Consumption, Adopt Sustainable Public Transportation, Eat a plant-rich diet, Conserve more water
